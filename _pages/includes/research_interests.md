# 🔬 Research Interests

- **Embodied AI and Navigation**: I am interested in agents that understand dynamic environments and make reliable decisions for navigation and interaction.

- **World Models with Spatio-temporal Priors**: I explore predictive representations that help agents reason over temporal structure, future states, and action-conditioned scene changes.

- **Computer Vision and Robot Control**: I focus on connecting visual representation learning with control pipelines for physical systems and embodied tasks.
